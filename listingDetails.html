More About Company

Welcome to the Wow Fashion School Pokhara. We are the best fashion designing institute in Pokhara. We provide a range of fashion designing courses in Pokhara. We are approved by both the CTEVT (Gov of Nepal) and ISO 9001:2015 (Globally Certified) and have been in business for over 20 years. In 2018/19 & 2022, we were awarded the best fashion school in Nepal. Our fashion school is led by international fashion designers from Cannes Film Festival, Tenzin Tseten Bhutia & Tara Gurung, in collaboration with professional mentors. Yet, education at Wow Fashion School goes beyond the classroom. With a firm belief in learning by doing, we are proud to offer 100% internship opportunities at our various branches and boutique.
